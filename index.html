Hotel Du Lac, Bellagio

Located in the best-rated area in Bellagio, this hotel has an excellent location score of 9.9. Hotel du Lac is set in the heart of Bellagio, overlooking the main square, opposite the dock. Here you can enjoy panoramic views and excellent food. This family-run hotel is passionately committed to making your stay on Lake Como unforgettable. The friendly team of staff are always on hand to share local knowledge and tourist information. Some rooms are larger and feature a terrace or balcony with partial or full lake views. Dining at du Lac is a real treat at the panoramic restaurant. Open at both lunch and dinner, it serves an eclectic mix of Italian and international cuisine.

Hotel La Perla, Tremezzo

La Perla Hotel provides panoramic views across Lake Como from its peaceful location in the hills above Tremezzo. It is surrounded by olive trees and has an outdoor pool. You can walk down to Tremezzo in around 15 minutes. Free parking is provided at Hotel La Perla. Most of La Perla's rooms have their own balcony and great views. All have flat-screen satellite TV and air conditioning. This quiet hotel is family-run and offers friendly service. There is a TV lounge, an internet point and a terrace bar.

Transportation

There are a lot of different ways to get around Lake Como without a car: private or public transportation will take you wherever you want. Buses, trains and boat service are a great way to move from town to town.

Bus service is a great way to move around Lake Como and save some money. If you travel by public transport, youâ€™ll be happy to know that bus stops are just outside the main train stations: there will always be a bus that will take you to your destination.

On the western branch of the lake, the city of Como is the only one to have train stations. If you are travelling to other towns of the western branch and do not want to move by taxi, you will have to continue your journey by bus or by boat.

Boat trips are recommended to anyone who wants to experience Lake Como at its best. Whether you are visiting Lake Como for a day or staying longer, jump aboard a boat, relax and reach your favorite destination: Bellagio, Varenna, Como, or one of the historical villas on the shores of the lake.
